Fogh Depot

Seductive brooding jazz with an electronic twist

Fogh Depot is a trio from Moscow, Russia, who makes music that incorporates elements of nu jazz, hip-hop, doom jazz and live-electronic. Baritone sax, double bass, drums, samplers and a whole lot of stomp boxes and effects.

"Melding electronic, ambient, modern classical and jazz, Fogh Depot create gorgeous textures and rhythms that are steeped in a primal mode of fusion similar to groups such as Hidden Orchestra, Xploding Plastix, The Cinematic Orchestra, Floex and others."

Sound Colour Vibration

"It’s an excellent mix of moody jazz and electronics that’s reminiscent of some of the great records released by Thirsty Ear a few years back."

Wax Poetics
